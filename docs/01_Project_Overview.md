# 🩺 MediVerify

## Project Overview

### Project Title

MediVerify – AI-Assisted Doctor Verified Second Opinion Platform

---

## Problem Statement

Many patients, especially in small cities and rural areas, continue medical treatments for months without knowing whether the diagnosis or treatment is appropriate. Access to experienced specialists is often expensive, time-consuming, or unavailable. This can lead to delayed recovery, unnecessary expenses, and poor health outcomes.

---

## Proposed Solution

MediVerify is an AI-assisted, doctor-verified second opinion platform.

Patients can upload their existing medical reports, prescriptions, and treatment history. AI organizes and summarizes the information, while verified specialists review the case and provide a professional second opinion.

The platform does not replace doctors. AI only assists in organizing information; the final recommendation is always provided by a verified medical professional.

---

## Objectives

- Help patients verify their ongoing treatment.
- Provide quick access to verified specialists.
- Reduce unnecessary medical expenses.
- Improve healthcare accessibility in underserved areas.
- Use AI to simplify medical report analysis.
- Build a secure and user-friendly healthcare platform.

---

## Target Users

- Patients seeking a second opinion
- People living in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities
- Chronic disease patients
- Elderly patients
- Working professionals
- Verified doctors and specialists

---

## Unique Selling Point (USP)

Unlike traditional telemedicine platforms that focus on starting new consultations, MediVerify focuses on reviewing ongoing treatments.

AI summarizes reports, while verified doctors provide the final medical recommendation.

---
